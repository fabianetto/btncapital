Investing in Uruguay

> <

  • Uruguay is a country with strong political and social stability, backed by a consolidated democracy and strong legal security. Respect for the political system and the essential fundamentals of economic activity are qualities that investors take into account when choosing Uruguay. Therefore, the country has a very favorable business climate that places it top in the region.

    (1 of 4)

  • Uruguay is placed in the top positions of Latin America - and in a very good position in the world - in political stability. It is a country with a strong democratic tradition, based on low rates of government corruption and a broad economic freedom.

    (2 of 4)

  • Uruguay is one of the main recipients of foreign direct investment (FDI) in South America, having reached record levels in the last decade. 65% of FDI profits are reinvested in the country, reflecting investor confidence, profitability and business climate.

    (3 of 4)

  • The foreign investor enjoys the same incentives as the local investor and there are no differences from the tax point of view. Uruguay guarantees equal treatment for investors and an open, non-restrictive operation regarding the mobility of capital and dividends as well as the purchase of local and foreign currency

    Source: Uruguay XXI
